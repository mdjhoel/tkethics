Idea Incubator

Many students are passionate about solving community issues and have innovative ideas, but lack the entrepreneurial experience and resources to bring them to life.

Idea Incubator provides mentorship from successful entrepreneurs, industry experts, and alumni. We connect students with professionals who can help turn their ideas into reality.

Key features

Open to individuals and teams aged 14+, the Idea Incubator is a hands on opportunity for creative students to learn real world skills working in teams on a practical project.

Mentors

Participants have access to experienced mentors, including successful entrepreneurs, industry experts, and TKEthics alumni. These mentors will provide guidance, feedback, and support throughout the program.

Workshops

Workshops on topics such as product development, market research, business models, pitching, and fundraising, which are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for startup success.

Hands on

Students will work on real projects, applying what they learn in a practical setting. This experiential learning approach helps them understand the intricacies of building and scaling a startup.

Networking

Building a strong network of peers, mentors and industry professionals is crucial for startup success. We facilitate connections that can open doors to future opportunities.

Elevator pitch

We provide dedicated sessions for students to practice and refine their business pitches. Constructive feedback from mentors and peers helps them improve their presentation skills and confidently communicate their ideas.

Demo day

The program culminates in a Demo Day, where participants present their refined startup ideas to a panel of judges, including potential investors and industry leaders. This event is an excellent opportunity for students to gain exposure and receive valuable feedback.
